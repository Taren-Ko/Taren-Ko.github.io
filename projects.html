  • whatshotOrderUp
    My final project for COMP 426, I worked with Kevin Jacoby to build this website. The website has 3 views. "Create Order" allows a customer to place an order to a restaurant by picking items and giving a name. "View Orders" is the cook view of the website and shows all orders and their status. Green for delivered, yellow for cooking, and red for not started. "Manage Menu" view allows the menu to be edited for a restaurant. Items can be added, edited, or deleted. The site uses Heroku which handles the menu items without the need of a proper database such as SQL. The design of the website uses the Bootstrap stylesheet. To view the website, click here. (Note: The site may take time to load due to us using the free version of Heroku)
