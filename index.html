Quick Bake Cake Cookies

For a treat that takes little effort for some great taste, look no further than these cookies! A simple recipe is provided for excellent Chocolate Chip cookies that are sure to satisfy any house guest!

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Yellow or Golden Cake Mix
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 Cup of Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Tablespoons of water
  • 6 oz. Bag of Chocolate Chips

Note: The chocolate chips in this recipe can be removed or replaced with peanut butter chips.

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a medium sized bowl.
  2. Take a teaspoon and scoop up a piece of batter and place it on a cookie sheet or pam sprayed tinfoil sheet on top of an oven pan.
  3. Repeat this, leaving roughly an inch and a half between blobs of batter. Make sure they are round and almost spherical in shape, but pressed down on the sheet.
  4. Bake at 300° F for about 6 to 8 minutes or until slightly brown.

Produces 36 Cookies!
